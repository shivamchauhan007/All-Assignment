3) What is the difference between Bootstrap 4 and Bootstrap 5
	-The form elements in Bootstrap 4 have defaulted to the browser-provided view.
	 However, in Bootstrap 5, the form elements have a custom design that enables them to have a consistent look and feel in all browsers.
	 The new form controls are based on completely semantic, standard form controls. This helps developers to avoid adding extra markups for form           controls.
